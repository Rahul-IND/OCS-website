COBRA

Welcome to the dynamic world of Siemens COBRA - where innovation converges with rail infrastructure excellence. In an era shaped by shifting markets and escalating global demands, Siemens Mobility Rail Infrastructure embarked on a visionary journey back in 2019. The birth of COBRA, standing for Competitive Operation Business for Rail Automation, marked the inception of a transformative endeavor. At its core, COBRA strives to establish a pioneering Center of Competence (CoC) for Project Execution, catering to both local and international aspirations.

COBRA's ultimate goal is twofold: to streamline project costs and mitigate risks, while catalyzing growth and profitability for Siemens Mobility Rail Infrastructure. As the global competitive landscape intensifies, our dedication to efficiency and cost-effectiveness remains steadfast. With a bold spirit and a commitment to excellence, COBRA has triumphed over challenges, transcending obstacles to achieve new pinnacles of success. Over the years, our steadfast team of over 250 colleagues has embodied dedication, underscoring our enduring commitment to innovation, global collaboration, and the relentless pursuit of excellence in shaping rail solutions that transcend borders and consistently exceed expectations.
